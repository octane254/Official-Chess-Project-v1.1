## How to Play

1. **Starting the Game**:
   - Click "New Game" to begin a fresh match
   - Choose game mode: Player vs AI 
   

2. **Making Moves**:
   - Click pieces to move them
   - Valid squares will highlight in green
   - Invalid moves will return pieces to their original position

3. **Game Controls**:
   - Restart Game
